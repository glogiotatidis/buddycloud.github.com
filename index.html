Channels

screenshot 1, screenshot 2, screenshot 3, screenshot 4

A channel is the place where you post your thoughts, pictures and videos. Have as many channels as you like: Share updates with millions of followers or with your family and closest friends only. Establish a community of experts or create a channel for one single event. You design your universe of channels the way you choose.

Strong Privacy

In buddycloud you really own your data. buddycloud’s strong encryption keep you safe online. And buddycloud is privacy made simple: There is only one place to check who can see your channel. Posting rights can easily be changed. Only you or your moderators approve who can follow your channel. And the best thing is: You can choose a provider you trust.

Drop-in Media Sharing

Drag any kind of file onto a channel to immediately share it with your followers. Quickly make an album of your holiday photos or upload that 700MB holiday video.

Channels in Your Pocket

Get quick channel updates directly on your Android and iPhone. Share photos to whatever channels of friends. Even better, buddycloud’s unique location sharing lets you bookmark places you visit regularly. Now you can automatically share where you were, where you are, and where you are going next with your trusted followers. 100% check-in free.

buddycloud for your team

buddycloud helps your team take an idea, explore it and make things happen.

You can keep information belonging to you or your team secure by running your own buddycloud inside your firewall. You control which other companies can connect to your buddycloud server.

buddycloud for developers

tools and magic to build something incredible

easy to set up

Simple to set up packages for major operating systems. A great guide to setup and run your own buddycloud server in 60 minutes. Your users simply log on to https://buddycloud.your-domain.com or setup your buddycloud server now.

plays well with others

buddycloud is simple to integrate with your existing network, your user database and your own products. Just drop the code into your project and add imagination.

open ecosystem

buddycloud is built by and for thinkers and makers who care about openness.

buddycloud is built on opensource software. All buddycloud code is ready for you to use in your own project. buddycloud uses a suite of modern open technologies like HTML5, node.js, and backbone.js. Learn more about developing on the buddycloud wiki.

buddycloud is built on open standards

buddycloud and the W3C, Mozilla Foundation, and XSF are working together to be the the strong foundation for an open social ecosystem.

Using open standards means that you don't have to depend on anyone else and can quickly finish your project using a wide range of free libraries.

what next?

Android, iPhone and Firefox OS

buddycloud knows how important mobile is. We have created a substantial framework for buddycloud on mobile devices. Now we are refining the Android and iPhone apps that bring you trusted location sharing and realtime updates with your buddycloud channels.

buddycloud library and API

To add social features quickly to your next project, the HTTP API and lib-buddycloud, a growing javascript library, will help you quickly build your own buddycloud client. Drop in, dead simple.

media server

We know media sharing must be drag-n-drop easy and just work. We’ve built a great media server so that you can share anything easily in your buddycloud channel. How to get started.
