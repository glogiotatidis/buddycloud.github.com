Distributed by design

Imagine users on gmail couldn't send email to a hotmail account. Sounds crazy and yet that's how social networks are designed.

buddycloud has designed a new way to share: each social network can connect and share information with other buddycloud websites. This means that your small website joins a much larger ecosystem of other domains also running buddycloud and users don't need to register again and again.

privacy

Strong privacy controls are backed into buddycloud. Servers and clients communicate using SSL encryption. Users to create private (or open) spaces to share content. and can control what data is shared with other sites running buddycloud.

media sharing

The buddycloud media server lets users drag and drop content into their buddycloud channels and seamlessly synchronised between all sites running buddycloud. This is a like a huge distributed DropBox. Each website that uses buddycloud can share pictures, videos and documents with users on other websites running buddycloud or publicly. Media can be shared with a selected group of users in a channel or the entire world. Learn more.

run your own buddycloud or have it hosted

We want you to be able to quickly start developing your own social features. You could install your own buddycloud stack: API, media, pusher, and buddycloud servers. Or, to get going even faster, we offer a free hosted solution that let's you "move out" your data when your needs grow and you decide to run your own buddycloud stack.

We make getting started easy - we can host your social API and later, when you have time, you can migrate out your data onto the same software that we host on.

recommendation engine

If you like the buddycloud channel food@foo.com then might be interenestd in omnomnom@bar.com. buddycloud includes a crawler with a nice API that helps your users find great content.

realtime search engine

Find channels about cooking or posts about siamese cats. Fast. Public content on buddycloud is quickly indexed and searchable and accessible via a standardised API and firehose feed.

Social discovery engine

The buddycloud friend finder helps your users find their friends on other social networks. The FF uses hashes of identifiers: for example Twitter or Facebook account IDs. This extends the usefulness to existing circles of friends.
